# Consumables

**Consumables** are [items](index.md) made typically via [cooking](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Cooking "Cooking") skill [recipes](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Cooking#Recipes "Cooking") or obtained through other [activities](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Activities "Activities") and [chests](chests.md).

To use a consumable on an [activity](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Activity "Activity"), you must be "using" enough of them to complete the action of the activity in steps. Example: Doing a single action of [Rescue Team](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Rescue_Team "Rescue Team"), where it ends up taking you personally 5667 steps to complete, would require 6 [Jelly Sandwiches](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Jelly_Sandwich "Jelly Sandwich") (6 x 1000 for 6000 steps of coverage).
