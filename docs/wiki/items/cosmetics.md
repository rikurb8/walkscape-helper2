# Cosmetics

Cosmetics are options to change the look of your character in Walkscape! Cosmetics have no in game use beside their visuals.

Cosmetics come in two types.

-   Clothing and things your character wears can be Outfits, Headwear, and Face, Back, and Eye accessories. Most of these can be purchased in a clothing store like [Tundra Trends](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Tundra_Trends "Tundra Trends").
-   The look of your character can be Faces, Noses, Hairstyles, and Facepaint as well as the color options for each. These can modified at a barber shop like [Polar Pruning](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Polar_Pruning "Polar Pruning").
