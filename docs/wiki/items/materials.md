# Materials

Materials are resource [items](index.md) that the player gathers from training the [Agility](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Agility "Agility"), [Foraging](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Foraging "Foraging"), [Fishing](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Fishing "Fishing"), [Mining](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Mining "Mining"), and [Woodcutting](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Woodcutting "Woodcutting") skills. These items are then used in the [Carpentry](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Carpentry "Carpentry"), [Cooking](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Cooking "Cooking"), [Crafting](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Crafting "Crafting"), [Smithing](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Smithing "Smithing"), [Trinketry](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Trinketry "Trinketry") skills to produce food, tools, weapons, shields, and jewelry. materials can also be received from [chests](chests.md).

Materials also have a [fine](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Fine_Material "Fine Material") version of themselves that can be be received in place of the regular version at a **base rate of 1/100**. These can be sold for a higher price to shops or allow better crafting quality chances.
