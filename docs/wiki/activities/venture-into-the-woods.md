# Venture into the woods

> You venture into the gloomy mists outside of Salsfirth, a dense network of foliage blocking your way to get into the woods.

The **Venture into the woods** [activity](index.md) is a [Foraging](../skills/foraging.md) activity. This is a [movement activity](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Movement_Activities "Special:MyLanguage/Movement Activities"), you will be teleported to [Witched Woods](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Witched_Woods "Special:MyLanguage/Witched Woods") upon completion of an action.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

-   [Salsfirth](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Salsfirth "Special:MyLanguage/Salsfirth") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

-   At least Foraging lvl. 50.

Keyword Requirements:

-   Requires **\[2\]** unique [Light source](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Light_source_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Light source Keyword") equipped.
