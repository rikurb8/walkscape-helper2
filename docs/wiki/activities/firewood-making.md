# Firewood making

> With patience, sweat, effort and your trusty hatchet, you split the logs mercilessly. If only you could split them with your bare hands. You'd have to be a superhero, to say the least.

The **Firewood making**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Woodcutting](../skills/woodcutting.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Coldington](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Coldington "Special:MyLanguage/Coldington") in  [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Woodcutting lvl. 15.
- At least  Agility lvl. 10.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have  [Hatchet](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Hatchet_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Hatchet Keyword") equipped.
