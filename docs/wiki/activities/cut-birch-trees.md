# Cut birch trees

> You swing your axe with all your might, determined to bring down the towering birch trees.

The **Cut birch trees** [activity](index.md) is a [Woodcutting](../skills/woodcutting.md) activity.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

-   [Disenchanted Forest](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Disenchanted_Forest "Special:MyLanguage/Disenchanted Forest") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")
-   [Nomad Woods](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Nomad_Woods "Special:MyLanguage/Nomad Woods") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

-   At least Woodcutting lvl. 1.

Keyword Requirements:

-   Have [Hatchet](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Hatchet_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Hatchet Keyword") equipped.
