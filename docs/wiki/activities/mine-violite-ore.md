# Mine violite ore

> You assess the violet rocks of hard violite in front of you and plot your path to best extract ores from them without hurting yourself in the process.

The **Mine violite ore**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Mining](../skills/mining.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Crown of Cinders](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Crown_of_Cinders "Special:MyLanguage/Crown of Cinders") in  [Wrentmark](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wrentmark "Special:MyLanguage/Wrentmark")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Mining lvl. 70.

Other Requirements:

- Have  [Pickaxe](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Pickaxe_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Pickaxe Keyword") equipped that requires at least  [Mining](../skills/mining.md) level **[60]**.
- While having  [Navigate Desert](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Abilities "Special:MyLanguage/Abilities") ability available.
