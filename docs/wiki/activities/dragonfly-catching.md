# Dragonfly catching

> You use your net to chase after and catch dragonflies that dart back and forth rapidly throughout the area.

The **Dragonfly catching**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Foraging](../skills/foraging.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Blackwater Fields](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Blackwater_Fields "Special:MyLanguage/Blackwater Fields") in  [Wallisia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia "Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Foraging lvl. 60.
- At least  Agility lvl. 50.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have  [Bug catching net](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Bug_catching_net_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Bug catching net Keyword") equipped.
