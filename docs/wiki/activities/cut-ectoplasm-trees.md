# Cut ectoplasm trees

> The trees here look like they are torn from another realm, their eerie look and the ghostly energy they exude makes them intimidating even before attempting to fell them. You are eager to process the logs they will bring.

The **Cut ectoplasm trees**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Woodcutting](../skills/woodcutting.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Wraithwater](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wraithwater "Special:MyLanguage/Wraithwater") in  [Wallisia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia "Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Woodcutting lvl. 55.

Other Requirements:

- Have  [Hatchet](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Hatchet_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Hatchet Keyword") equipped that requires at least  [Woodcutting](../skills/woodcutting.md) level **[50]**.
