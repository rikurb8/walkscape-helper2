# Wine grape stomping

> With bare feet so as not to break the seeds, you step on the grapes. You move from the center of the press towards the ends, thus facilitating fermentation, since the pressure exerted on the fruit allows contact with the yeasts in charge of the process.

The **Wine grape stomping** [activity](index.md) is a [Cooking](../skills/cooking.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Granfiddich](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Granfiddich "Special:MyLanguage/Granfiddich") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Cooking lvl. 45.
- At least Agility lvl. 30.
