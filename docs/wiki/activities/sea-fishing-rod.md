# Sea fishing (Rod)

> Along the shoreline, you find a secluded spot and cast your rod into the calm waters, enjoying the peaceful solitude of sea fishing.

The **Sea fishing (Rod)**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Fishing](../skills/fishing.md) activity.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

- [Granfiddich Shores](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Granfiddich_Shores "Special:MyLanguage/Granfiddich Shores") in  [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
- [Port Skildar](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Port_Skildar "Special:MyLanguage/Port Skildar") in  [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Fishing lvl. 20.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have  [Fishing rod](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Fishing_rod_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Fishing rod Keyword") equipped.
