# Cut willow trees

> The wide trunks of these willows are proving to be a challenge, but with your skillful and powerful swings you manage to topple them one by one.

The **Cut willow trees** [activity](index.md) is a [Woodcutting](../skills/woodcutting.md) activity.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

- [Warrenfield](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Warrenfield "Special:MyLanguage/Warrenfield") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
- [Witched Woods](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Witched_Woods "Special:MyLanguage/Witched Woods") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Woodcutting lvl. 40.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have [Hatchet](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Hatchet_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Hatchet Keyword") equipped.
