# Bodysurfing

> Before entering the water, you check the conditions: wind magnitude and direction, presence and size of rocks, presence of fishermen, boats or dangerous animals. You've even chatted with the locals. Everything is right. You go for a swim among the waves.

The **Bodysurfing** [activity](index.md) is a [Agility](../skills/agility.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

-   [Red Coast](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Red_Coast "Special:MyLanguage/Red Coast") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
