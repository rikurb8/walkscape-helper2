# Graveyard foraging

> You tread through the graveyard and look for unique things out of place that have come loose from the floods in the area. It isn't grave robbing if it isn't still in the grave, right?

The **Graveyard foraging** [activity](index.md) is a [Foraging](../skills/foraging.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Wraithwater](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wraithwater "Special:MyLanguage/Wraithwater") in [Wallisia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia "Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirement

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Foraging lvl. 55.
