# Hull repair

> You are no longer an apprentice carpenter, so you repair larger vessels without a problem. Your foreman trusts your skills and good judgment. You know when to be effective and when to be efficient. Your toolkit is professional, just like you.

The **Hull repair** [activity](index.md) is a [Carpentry](../skills/carpentry.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

-   [Blackspell Port](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Blackspell_Port "Special:MyLanguage/Blackspell Port") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

-   At least Carpentry lvl. 40.
-   At least Smithing lvl. 30.

Keyword Requirements:

-   Have [Carpentry tool](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry_tool_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry tool Keyword") equipped.
