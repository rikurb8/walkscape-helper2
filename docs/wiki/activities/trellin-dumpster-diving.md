# Trellin dumpster diving

> Amidst the labyrinthine alleyways of the city, you explore the forgotten corners, sifting through discarded odds and ends in hopes of unearthing hidden treasures among the cast-offs.

The **Trellin dumpster diving**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Foraging](../skills/foraging.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Granfiddich](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Granfiddich "Special:MyLanguage/Granfiddich") in  [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Foraging lvl. 35.
- At least  Agility lvl. 20.
