# Kayaking

> You check the paddles, seat and general condition of the kayak before putting it in the water. On yourself, helmet and waterproof clothing. The weather couldn't be better: sunshine, pleasant temperature and a light breeze. Let's enjoy a good paddling session!

The **Kayaking**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Agility](../skills/agility.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Old Arena Ruins](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Old_Arena_Ruins "Special:MyLanguage/Old Arena Ruins") in  [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Agility lvl. 30.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have  [Life vest](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Life_vest_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Life vest Keyword") equipped.
