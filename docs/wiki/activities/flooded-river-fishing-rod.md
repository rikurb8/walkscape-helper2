# Flooded river fishing (Rod)

> You cast your fishing rod into the flooded river waters, hoping to catch some unique fish that have adapted to their new environment.

The **Flooded river fishing (Rod)** [activity](index.md) is a [Fishing](../skills/fishing.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Tendon Wet Fields](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Tendon_Wet_Fields "Special:MyLanguage/Tendon Wet Fields") in [Wallisia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia "Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Fishing lvl. 55.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have [Fishing rod](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Fishing_rod_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Fishing rod Keyword") equipped.
