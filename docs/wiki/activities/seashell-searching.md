# Seashell searching

> You search carefully along the coast for any valuable seashells or pearls.

The **Seashell searching** [activity](index.md) is a [Foraging](../skills/foraging.md) activity.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

- [Beach of Woes](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Beach_of_Woes "Special:MyLanguage/Beach of Woes") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")
- [Farsand Coast](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Farsand_Coast "Special:MyLanguage/Farsand Coast") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
- [Granfiddich Shores](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Granfiddich_Shores "Special:MyLanguage/Granfiddich Shores") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirement

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Foraging lvl. 25.

# Trivia

- This is often used as a lower level money making method due to the value of the items and chests obtained from this activity along with its close proximity to a bank.
- This was the first activity to be able to drop [Sunken chest](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Sunken_chest "Special:MyLanguage/Sunken chest").
