# Emergency darkness escape (Swamp)

> You've lost your light source and will have to find your way out of where you are through the dark. You must be careful to make your way out and avoid any dangerous creatures that may lurk in the shadows.

The **Emergency darkness escape (Swamp)** [activity](index.md) is a [Agility](../skills/agility.md) activity. This is a [movement activity](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Movement_Activities "Special:MyLanguage/Movement Activities"), you will be teleported to [Old Arena Ruins](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Old_Arena_Ruins "Special:MyLanguage/Old Arena Ruins") upon completion of an action.

# Locations

- [Bog Bottom](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Bog_Bottom "Special:MyLanguage/Bog Bottom") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
- [Bog Top](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Bog_Top "Special:MyLanguage/Bog Top") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
- [Halfling Campgrounds](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Halfling_Campgrounds "Special:MyLanguage/Halfling Campgrounds") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This is an **emergency** activity. It is only visible if you _don't_ meet the following keyword requirements:

- Requires **\[2\]** unique [Light source](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Light_source_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Light source Keyword") equipped.

# Requirement

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Agility lvl. 1.
