# Ice sculpting

> Armed with ice shaping tools and saws, you transform an ice cube into something else, either functional or artistic. The fleetness of this piece of art is proportional to the ambient temperature, its duration ranging from minutes to permanent.

The **Ice sculpting** [activity](index.md) is a [Carpentry](../skills/carpentry.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

-   [Frusenholm](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Frusenholm "Special:MyLanguage/Frusenholm") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

-   At least Carpentry lvl. 15.
-   At least Crafting lvl. 15.

Keyword Requirements:

-   Have [Carpentry tool](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry_tool_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry tool Keyword") equipped.
