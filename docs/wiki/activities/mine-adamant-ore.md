# Mine adamant ore

> Finding the tough adamant rocks before you, you bring your best equipment out in attempts to remove the ore from the rock.

The **Mine adamant ore** [activity](index.md) is a [Mining](../skills/mining.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Crown of Cinders](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Crown_of_Cinders "Special:MyLanguage/Crown of Cinders") in [Wrentmark](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wrentmark "Special:MyLanguage/Wrentmark")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Mining lvl. 60.

Other Requirements:

- Have [Pickaxe](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Pickaxe_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Pickaxe Keyword") equipped that requires at least [Mining](../skills/mining.md) level **\[50\]**.
- While having [Navigate Desert](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Abilities "Special:MyLanguage/Abilities") ability available.
