# Brig repair

> You take part in repairs due to wear and tear of small vessels. Timbers, nails, rigging, fittings and ropes are at your disposal. The foreman gives the orders and you execute them masterfully. Maybe one day you will be the one giving the orders.

The **Brig repair** [activity](index.md) is a [Carpentry](../skills/carpentry.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Port Skildar](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Port_Skildar "Special:MyLanguage/Port Skildar") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Carpentry lvl. 20.
- At least Smithing lvl. 10.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have [Carpentry tool](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry_tool_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry tool Keyword") equipped.
