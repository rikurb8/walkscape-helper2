# Run for your life

> You feel claustrophobic as the darkness closes in around you and feel like you are being watched. You start to run for your life, with no sense of direction.

The **Run for your life** [activity](index.md) is a [Agility](../skills/agility.md) activity. This is a [movement activity](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Movement_Activities "Special:MyLanguage/Movement Activities"), you will be teleported to [Old Arena Ruins](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Old_Arena_Ruins "Special:MyLanguage/Old Arena Ruins") upon completion of an action.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

-   [Bog Top](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Bog_Top "Special:MyLanguage/Bog Top") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
-   [Halfling Campgrounds](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Halfling_Campgrounds "Special:MyLanguage/Halfling Campgrounds") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
-   [Halfmaw Hideout](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Halfmaw_Hideout "Special:MyLanguage/Halfmaw Hideout") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
-   [Witched Woods](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Witched_Woods "Special:MyLanguage/Witched Woods") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

Keyword Requirements:
