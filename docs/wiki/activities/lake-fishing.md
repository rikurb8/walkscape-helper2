# Lake fishing

> You carefully attach your bait with frosty fingers and cast it out upon the calm surface of the lake.

The **Lake fishing** [activity](index.md) is a [Fishing](../skills/fishing.md) activity.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

-   [Azurazera](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Azurazera "Special:MyLanguage/Azurazera") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")
-   [Frusenholm](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Frusenholm "Special:MyLanguage/Frusenholm") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

-   At least Fishing lvl. 5.

Keyword Requirements:

-   Have [Fishing rod](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Fishing_rod_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Fishing rod Keyword") equipped.
