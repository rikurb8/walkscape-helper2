# Venture into the hideout

> You venture into the gloomy mists outside of Blackspell Port, thick roots and even trees through the marshes blocking your way into the hideout.

The **Venture into the hideout** [activity](index.md) is a [Woodcutting](../skills/woodcutting.md) activity. This is a [movement activity](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Movement_Activities "Special:MyLanguage/Movement Activities"), you will be teleported to [Halfmaw Hideout](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Halfmaw_Hideout "Special:MyLanguage/Halfmaw Hideout") upon completion of an action.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Blackspell Port](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Blackspell_Port "Special:MyLanguage/Blackspell Port") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Woodcutting lvl. 50.

Keyword Requirements:

- Requires **\[2\]** unique [Light source](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Light_source_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Light source Keyword") equipped.
