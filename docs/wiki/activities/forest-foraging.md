# Forest foraging

> Amidst the winter forest, you venture with a keen eye, foraging for hidden treasures beneath the blanket of snow, embracing the thrill of discovering nature's edible offerings.

The **Forest foraging**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Foraging](../skills/foraging.md) activity.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

- [Disenchanted Forest](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Disenchanted_Forest "Special:MyLanguage/Disenchanted Forest") in  [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")
- [Horn of Respite](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Horn_of_Respite "Special:MyLanguage/Horn of Respite") in  [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirement

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Foraging lvl. 1.
