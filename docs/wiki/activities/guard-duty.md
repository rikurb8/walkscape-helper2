# Guard duty

> As a guard in the Wallisian Empire, you've got bills to pay, and this is how you do it. You watch over the kingdom, ensuring you get paid for your watchful eyes.

The **Guard duty** [activity](index.md) is a [Agility](../skills/agility.md) activity.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

- [Bilgemont Port](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Bilgemont_Port "Special:MyLanguage/Bilgemont Port") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
- [Blackspell Port](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Blackspell_Port "Special:MyLanguage/Blackspell Port") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
- [Everhaven](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Everhaven "Special:MyLanguage/Everhaven") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")
- [Warrenfield](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Warrenfield "Special:MyLanguage/Warrenfield") in [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Agility lvl. 55.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have [Light source](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Light_source_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Light source Keyword") equipped.
- Have [Shield](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Shield_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Shield Keyword") equipped.
- Have [Weapon](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Weapon_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Weapon Keyword") equipped.

Other Requirements:

- Have **\[40\]** [Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Erdwise") faction reputation.
