# Flooded field scavenging

> You wade through the wet fields in search of viable plants and materials that have managed to survive and even thrive in this damp environment.

The **Flooded field scavenging**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Foraging](../skills/foraging.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Tendon Wet Fields](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Tendon_Wet_Fields "Special:MyLanguage/Tendon Wet Fields") in  [Wallisia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia "Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirement

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Foraging lvl. 55.
