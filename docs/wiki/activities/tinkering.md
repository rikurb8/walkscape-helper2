# Tinkering

> You scratch your chin as you think about how you can fix this junk. You come up with a thousand ways to do it, but for most of them you're missing components. You look around the workshop, searching for something specific. There it is! Let's get to work.

The **Tinkering** [activity](index.md) is a [Crafting](../skills/crafting.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Barbantok](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Barbantok "Special:MyLanguage/Barbantok") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")
