# Mountain climbing

> You prepare for your climb. It is a beautiful day with sunshine and no wind. Still, as you slowly climb, your heart skips a beat and you lose grip on one foot. What a scare! Finally you reach the top and look proudly at the world beneath your feet.

The **Mountain climbing** [activity](index.md) is a [Agility](../skills/agility.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

-   [Frostbite Mountain](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Frostbite_Mountain "Special:MyLanguage/Frostbite Mountain") in [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

-   At least Agility lvl. 10.

Keyword Requirements:

-   Have [Climbing gear](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Climbing_gear_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Climbing gear Keyword") equipped.
