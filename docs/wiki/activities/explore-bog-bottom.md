# Explore bog bottom

> You follow your underwater map and explore the hidden path in the murky depths at the bottom of the bog. They seem to twist and turn endlessly, but they are leading you somewhere. You just have to keep swimming.

The **Explore bog bottom**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Mining](../skills/mining.md) activity.
This is a [movement activity](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Movement_Activities "Special:MyLanguage/Movement Activities"), you will be teleported to  [Underwater Cave](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Underwater_Cave "Special:MyLanguage/Underwater Cave") upon completion of an action.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Bog Bottom](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Bog_Bottom "Special:MyLanguage/Bog Bottom") in  [Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Grand_Duchy_of_Trellin-Erdwise "Special:MyLanguage/Grand Duchy of Trellin-Erdwise")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Mining lvl. 50.

Keyword Requirements:

- Requires **[2]** unique  [Light source](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Light_source_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Light source Keyword") equipped.
- Requires **[3]** unique  [Advanced diving gear](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Advanced_diving_gear_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Advanced diving gear Keyword") equipped.

Other Requirements:

- Have the  [Underwater map](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Underwater_map "Special:MyLanguage/Underwater map") collectible.
