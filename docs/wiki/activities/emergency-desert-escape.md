# Emergency desert escape

> You've lost your camel and will soon succumb to the desert elements. You will have to wander out from the harsh environment however possible.

The **Emergency desert escape** [activity](index.md) is a [Agility](../skills/agility.md) activity. This is a [movement activity](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Movement_Activities "Special:MyLanguage/Movement Activities"), you will be teleported to [Kildome Cross](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Kildome_Cross "Special:MyLanguage/Kildome Cross") upon completion of an action.

# Locations

This activity can be done at the following locations:

- [Crown of Cinders](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Crown_of_Cinders "Special:MyLanguage/Crown of Cinders") in [Wrentmark](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wrentmark "Special:MyLanguage/Wrentmark")
- [Myriadian Arc](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Myriadian_Arc "Special:MyLanguage/Myriadian Arc") in [Wrentmark](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wrentmark "Special:MyLanguage/Wrentmark")

# Visibility

This is an **emergency** activity.

# Requirement

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Agility lvl. 1.
