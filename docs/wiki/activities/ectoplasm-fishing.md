# Ectoplasm fishing

> The water here glows in strange and eerie hues that radiate with a ghostly energy. You wonder what the fish here eat and if you are even using the proper tool to catch them. Your mind wanders to the attempt of trying to cook them later to see if they are even edible.

The **Ectoplasm fishing** [activity](index.md) is a [Fishing](../skills/fishing.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Wraithwater](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wraithwater "Special:MyLanguage/Wraithwater") in [Wallisia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia "Special:MyLanguage/Wallisia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least Fishing lvl. 55.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have [Fishing tool](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Fishing_tool_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Fishing tool Keyword") equipped.
