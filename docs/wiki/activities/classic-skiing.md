# Classic skiing

> On your beginner cross-country skiing journey, every glide is filled with wonder. The snowy trails are your playground, where you learn the rhythm of the skis and embrace the serene beauty of the winter woods.

The **Classic skiing**  [activity](index.md) is a  [Agility](../skills/agility.md) activity.

# Location

This activity can be done at the following location:

- [Casbrant Fields](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Casbrant_Fields "Special:MyLanguage/Casbrant Fields") in  [Jarvonia](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia "Special:MyLanguage/Jarvonia")

# Visibility

This activity is always visible.

# Requirements

Skill Level Requirements:

- At least  Agility lvl. 5.

Keyword Requirements:

- Have  [Skis](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Skis_Keyword "Special:MyLanguage/Skis Keyword") equipped.
