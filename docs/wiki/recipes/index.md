# Recipes

Recipes are ways to transform materials into refined versions ([Logs](../skills/carpentry.md) and [Bars](../skills/smithing.md)), cook [consumables](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/consumables "Special:MyLanguage/consumables"), and create [tools and items](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Equipment "Special:MyLanguage/Equipment").

Recipes often have a [service](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Services "Special:MyLanguage/Services") requirement in addition to the raw materials.

Recipes involve the [Carpentry](../skills/carpentry.md), [Cooking](../skills/cooking.md), [Crafting](../skills/crafting.md), [Smithing](../skills/smithing.md), and [Trinketry](../skills/trinketry.md) skills.

These skills can also benefit from a bonus of +1 [Crafting Outcome](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Crafting_Outcome "Special:MyLanguage/Crafting Outcome") per level beyond a Recipe's level requirement toward [crafted items](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/crafted_items "Special:MyLanguage/crafted items") with no cap.
