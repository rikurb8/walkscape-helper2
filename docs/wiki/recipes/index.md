# Recipes

Recipes are ways to transform materials into refined versions ([Logs](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry "Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry") and [Bars](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Smithing "Special:MyLanguage/Smithing")), cook [consumables](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/consumables "Special:MyLanguage/consumables"), and create [tools and items](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Equipment "Special:MyLanguage/Equipment").

Recipes often have a [service](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Services "Special:MyLanguage/Services") requirement in addition to the raw materials.

Recipes involve the [Carpentry](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry "Special:MyLanguage/Carpentry"), [Cooking](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Cooking "Special:MyLanguage/Cooking"), [Crafting](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Crafting "Special:MyLanguage/Crafting"), [Smithing](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Smithing "Special:MyLanguage/Smithing"), and [Trinketry](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Trinketry "Special:MyLanguage/Trinketry") skills.

These skills can also benefit from a bonus of +1 [Crafting Outcome](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Crafting_Outcome "Special:MyLanguage/Crafting Outcome") per level beyond a Recipe's level requirement toward [crafted items](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/crafted_items "Special:MyLanguage/crafted items") with no cap.
