> The sturdy bone handle feels light even with the thick ectoplasm head on the end of it, which feels squishy when it isn't being swung around and being used as a smithing tool.

### Craft a spectral hammer

> You bundle bone together to form a strong grip and work the ectoplasm into a block that you mold onto the end of the grip. Thus creating a thick hammer head for it.

Work bone and ectoplasm together to create a ghostly smithing tool.
