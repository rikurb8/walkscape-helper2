# Craft a spectral hatchet

[Normal](#tabber-Normal)[Good](#tabber-Good)[Great](#tabber-Great)[Excellent](#tabber-Excellent)[Perfect](#tabber-Perfect)[Eternal](#tabber-Eternal)

> A cluster of bone given power with ectoplasm to take the form of a hatchet. It doesn't look it, since it is somewhat transparent, but its blade is quite sharp.

The **Spectral hatchet** [item](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Items "Special:MyLanguage/Items") is a [crafted item](https://wiki.walkscape.app/wiki/Special:MyLanguage/Crafted "Special:MyLanguage/Crafted").
