## Primary Recipe Output

### Craft an adamant hatchet

> Rather than strike the adamant this time, you decide to take it to dinner. Unfortunately it doesn't have a good time, since it is a bar of metal. Reluctantly you turn it into a hatchet head and affix it to a yew handle. You will always remember the time you had together.

Craft adamant and yew together to create a woodcutting hatchet.
