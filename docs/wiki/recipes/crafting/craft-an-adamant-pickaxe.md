## Primary Recipe Output

### Craft an adamant pickaxe

> You attempt to force the adamant into the shape of pickaxe head. It fights you a little, but eventually takes form. You almost forgot you had to deal with the yew, but it too falls to your command, taking shape as a new handle. Together they fit to become a new adamant pickaxe.

Craft adamant and yew together to create a mining pickaxe.
